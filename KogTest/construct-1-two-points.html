How fast is the curve climbing between two points?

Drag the two points A and B along the curve. The straight line through them shows how steeply the curve climbs across that stretch — how much it goes up for each step across.

First, predict

Before you drag: what is the steepness between x = 1 and x = 3? Lock in a guess, then drag A and B there to check.

Climb across this stretch
—
Drag A and B to compare different stretches of the curve.

Investigate

  • Notice — does the climb stay the same as you move the points? A straight line has one steepness everywhere. Does this curve?
  • Predict — where would you put A and B to get the steepest stretch? The flattest?
  • Wonder — what would the number describe if A and B were almost on top of each other?
