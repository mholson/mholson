A step of size h

Start at a point on the curve y = x². Take a step across of size h to a nearby point — to the right (h positive) or the left (h negative). Each step has a rise, a run, and so a steepness.

First, predict

Keep x = 2. As the step h shrinks to almost nothing — from either side — what single steepness do you think the step heads toward?

steepness of this step = rise ÷ run
